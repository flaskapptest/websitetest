About Me

Alex Mednis

This website was created to host my resume and to learn how to create a simple app with the Flask framework.

In my free time I enjoy volunteering. More recently I began volunteering at a senior’s residence. With this position my typical key responsibilities are to interact with clients, assist them when needed in the activity being facilitated and insuring client’s wellbeing is being met. Through this opportunity I have learned I enjoy working with seniors in this respect. Other tasks I have completed were scraping the internet with a simple script to fill in information for their movie collection, as well as filling out birthday cards with a program from Microsoft Office Suite.

In the future I would be interested in pursuing employment in the food industry in a residential home setting. In pursuit of this goal I have attended cooking classes that are hosted at the grocery store, trying out and making different cuisines and cooking styles. I have recently gotten my safe food handler’s certificate towards this end. In my free time I enjoy researching recipes and recreating them as well as attending a local cooking class occasionally.

Living on a hobby farm, I’ve been given the opportunity to regularly care for animals; taking my dogs for a walk, and watching my goats roam, and collecting eggs from my chickens. Sugar gliders are of a particular interest to me, although I have opted not to buy any yet as owning them is a full time job in and of itself.

Along with my safe food handler certificate, I also earned my smartserve and cpr. I am open to further courses to improve my skill set or enhance my education, especially in the realms of cooking, animal care, and computers.

Thanks for taking the time to read this, any questions or comments can be directed at my email mednisalex@gmail.com
